Hi! I'm Eduardo

Currently, I am doing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Örebro University. My research is funded by Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program (WASP), one of the largest doctoral program in Sweden.

My research delves into the field of Affective Computing. My focus is to use AI methods to address the challenges found in monitoring affective states on a daily basis, for example, affective priming.
Throughout my research, I have worked with physiological-based signals by using wearable sensors, such as the Empatica E4 sensor.
